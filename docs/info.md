Workshop on Quantum Algorithms
Date: 13.2.2025 (Friday)
Venue: IISER Pune
The aim of the workshop is to introduce quantum algorithms to PhD students, Post-
doctoral fellows, and industry researchers in research labs and industry. The
workshop would cover a range of basic to advanced algorithms, including a hands-
on session. This will be useful to those who are working or plan to work on quantum
computing and its applications in industry.
Speakers:
Prof. Apoorva Patel (IISC, Bengaluru)
Prof. Prabha Mandyam (IIT Madras)
Dr. V. S. Prasanna (TCG Crest – Kolkata)
M. S. Santhanam (IISER Pune)
Number of participants:
50 (students / postdocs) + 25 (industry)
Plan of the workshop:
1.5 hours lecture by each speaker followed by some hands-on practical sessions
Fee for the workshop:
PhD Students (Rs 2000.00)
Post-doctoral fellows (Rs 3000.00)
Industry and national research labs (Rs 10000.00)
Tentative topics for speakers
Prof. Apoorva Patel (Introduction to quantum algorithms, some basics)
Prof. Prabha Mandyam (Factoring, Order finding, Shor’s algorithms etc.)
Prof. MS Santhanam ( lecture and hand-on practicals: TBD)
Dr. Prasanna Srinivas(HHL algorithm and its applications)
